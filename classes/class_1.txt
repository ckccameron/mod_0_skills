Class: Vegetable

Attributes:
color (string)
days_old (integer)
shipment_weight (float)
price_per_pound (float)
round_shape (boolean)

Methods:
overcook (changes the color string)
new_order (decreases the days_old integer)
increase_order (increases the shipment_weight float)
introduce_drought (increases the price_per_pound float)
cut_vegetable (makes round_shape boolean false)
