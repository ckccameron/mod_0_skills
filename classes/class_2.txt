Class: Restaurant_Staff

Attributes:
weekly_hours_worked (float)
total_pay_per_hour (float)
on_time (boolean)
earn_tips (boolean)

Methods:
take_vacation (decreases the weekly_hours_worked float)
earn_promotion (increases total_pay_per_hour float)
call_in_sick (makes the on_time boolean false)
bartend (makes earn_tips boolean true)
